The bread

Our traditional, organic sourdough bread handcrafted from the best ingredients, is the continuation of a family tradition. Each loaf undergoes a lenghty fermentation with natural sourdough and a small amount of baker's yeast, without adding any additives or preservatives, resulting in a delicious, and nutritious product.

Every loaf of our bread is unique, handcrafted by a baker who dedicates his time and passion to creating a product that nourishes both body and soul. Enjoy the true taste of artisanal and organic sourdough bread!
